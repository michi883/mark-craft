About Mark Craft

What is Mark Craft?

Mark Craft is an AI-powered logo generator that transforms your product description into professional brand identities in seconds. Perfect for startups, hackathons, and rapid prototyping.

Value Proposition

  • Instant Results: Get professional logos in seconds, not hours
  • Smart Branding: AI extracts keywords and tone from your description
  • Multiple Concepts: Explore 3 unique design directions before refining
  • Professional Output: Export clean SVG files ready for any use case
